Background

Before this project, Teams allowed users to chat with coworkers interally, as well as external collaborators at other organizations. In 2020, Teams for personal use was released and allowed customers to chat and video call with friends, family, or anyone else using a personal account. Microsoft then announced that Teams Chat for personal use would come pre-installed with the new Windows 11, with the potential to reach over 1 billion existing Windows customers.
