Who I am

I'm a designer by trade, but I'm most passionate about building great products, whether that's creating pixel-perfect mocks, writing code, or project planning. Right now, I design tools to help creators build their businesses at Patreon.

Before that, I was the founding designer at Prepared, a startup focused on innovating 911 emergency response technology. I joined Prepared as employee #15 and led product design as the company grew from seed to Series B. Previously, I've also designed for the Teams Education product at Microsoft Teams.


Once upon a time, I studied Computer Science and Human-Centered Design at Dartmouth College. That's where I discovered my passion for design while developing the technical skills that allow me to bridge design and engineering today.
